    About me

    Hi, my name's Boris. I major in Communication Arts at CCSA in Delaware. I am a 16-year-old student who is passionate about art, design, and technology. I have a keen interest in graphic design, web development, and digital art. I enjoy creating visually appealing designs and exploring new technologies to enhance my skills.

    I am currently working on a personal portfolio website to showcase my work and projects. I believe that a strong online presence is essential for any creative professional, and I am excited to share my journey with others.

    In my free time, I enjoy exploring new design trends, experimenting with different art styles, and collaborating with other creatives. I am always looking for opportunities to learn and grow as an artist and designer.
